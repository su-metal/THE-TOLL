# THE TOLL Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last updated: 2026-02-13

This Privacy Policy explains how THE TOLL ("we", "our", "us") handles personal data when you use the THE TOLL Chrome extension and smartphone web app (the "Service").

## 1. Data We Process
### 1.1 Account and Identity Data
- Email address and account identifiers from Google/Supabase authentication.

### 1.2 Billing Data
- Stripe customer ID, subscription status, billing lifecycle metadata (for example: cancellation at period end).
- Payment card details are processed by Stripe, not stored directly by us.

### 1.3 Service Usage and Configuration Data
- Extension settings such as blocked domains, schedule, target reps, and related feature toggles.
- Session IDs and unlock state used to coordinate lock/unlock flows between browser and smartphone app.

### 1.4 Technical and Security Data
- Basic request and function logs required for troubleshooting and abuse prevention.

## 2. How We Use Data
- Provide core functionality (website lock, exercise unlock, account state sync).
- Manage subscriptions and access levels (free/trial/pro).
- Detect and resolve operational issues.
- Maintain security and prevent abuse.

## 3. Legal Basis (if applicable)
- Contractual necessity: to provide the Service requested by the user.
- Legitimate interests: security, reliability, and abuse prevention.
- Consent where required by applicable law.

## 4. Data Sharing
We share data only with service providers needed to operate the Service:
- Google (authentication)
- Stripe (billing and subscription management)
- Supabase (database/auth/backend operations)
- Hosting and infrastructure vendors used by the web app

We do not sell personal data.

## 5. Data Retention
- Account and billing linkage data is retained while your account is active and for up to 180 days after account deletion, unless a longer period is required by law.
- Operational logs are retained for 30 to 90 days depending on platform and operational needs.
- Data required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or fraud prevention may be retained longer as required by applicable law.

## 6. User Controls
- You can cancel subscription through Stripe Customer Portal.
- You can stop using the extension/app and remove local extension data via browser settings.
- You may request deletion/correction/export of account-related data via contact channel below.

## 7. International Transfers
- Data may be processed in regions where our service providers operate.
- We rely on provider safeguards for cross-border processing where required.

## 8. Children
- The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent local age threshold).

## 9. Security
- We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data.
- No method of storage/transmission is 100% secure.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy and will update the "Last updated" date.
